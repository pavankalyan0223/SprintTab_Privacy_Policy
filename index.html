SprintTab Privacy Policy

SprintTab does not collect, transmit, or share any user data.

All data created by SprintTab — including saved sprint items, clipped text, sticky notes, page URLs, and search index content — is stored exclusively in your browser using Chrome's local storage APIs (chrome.storage.local and IndexedDB). This data never leaves your device.

SprintTab does not make any network requests. There are no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services involved.

What is stored locally

  • URLs and titles of pages you save to your sprint list
  • Text you highlight and clip from web pages
  • Sticky note content you write
  • Your notepad text

Who can access this data

Only you, on your device. Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.

Content script access

SprintTab's content scripts run on web pages you visit in order to enable text clipping (Alt+C) and highlight re-injection. No page content is read or transmitted without explicit user action.
